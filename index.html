dalgona coffee recipe

this yummy south korean coffee treat that's all over the internet only requires 4 ingredients to make-all of which you probably have in the cupboard-you feel like you won the jackpot right?

ingredients

  • Instant coffee powder or expresso granules
  • hot water
  • milk
  • sugar

dalgona making process

step 1: combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons(tbsp) of instant coffee , 2 tbsp of sugar and 2 tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl
step 2: whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.
step 3: serve with milk
Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed. Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
